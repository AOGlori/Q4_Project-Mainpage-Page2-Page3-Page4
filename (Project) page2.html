    • Cristomo Ibarra
    He is the son of the late Don Rafael
    He is also a childhood friend/sweetheart to Maria Clara

    Maria Clara
    She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and her late mother Pia Alba

    Kapitan Tiago
    He is the father of Maria Clara and the husband of late Pia Alba and a influential buissnes man in San Diego

    Padre Damaso
    He is a francisian priest in San Diego and the godfather of Maria Clara and a friend to Kapitan Tiago

    Basilio
    He is the eldest son of Sisa and the older brother of Crispin
    He also played a major role in the sequel
    He alongside his brother work as a sacristans at the San Diego parish

    Crispin
    He is the younger son of Sisa and the younger brother of Basilio
    He was killed by the cheif sexton for stealing money

    Sisa
    She is the mother of Basilio and Crispin.
    She was also abused by her husband
